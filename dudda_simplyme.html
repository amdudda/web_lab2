Where To Find Me?

I enjoy reading copious amounts of science fiction. My favorite authors include C.J. Cherryh and Seanan McGuire.

I am a Software Develompent student at Minneapolis Community and Technical College. I took Java last semester, and am enrolled in C#.Net and a Web Programming class this term.

I also enjoy travelling. I've been to such far-flung places as New Zealand and Cyprus. My last international trip was to London for Worldcon in 2014, held at the Excel Centre in the London Docklands.

I knit and do other crafty things! I have a very small shop on Etsy where I have utterly failed to sell anything, but one may always hope... sigh

Lots of deaf people enjoy music, and I'm no exception. I used to play the cello, and I have taken up the tenor recorder. I'm currently obsessed with Bread and Roses (PDF), which I first encountered in the movie Pride.

I am a polyglot and amateur linguist. I speak English, German, American Sign Language, some French, and a tiny smattering of Mandarin Chinese. I love learning about how languages are put together and some of the stunningly diverse ways people can think about and talk about the universe.
